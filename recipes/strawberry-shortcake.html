Strawberry Shortcake

Description

Homemade strawberry shortcake recipe made with sweet, sugary berries.

Serve with whipped cream on top

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 4 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1 cup cold butter, cubed
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 3 cups sliced fresh strawberries
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F or 175 degrees C
  2. Mix togethor flour, 1/4 cup sugar, baking powder, and cream of tartar in a large bowl. Cut in butter with a pastry blender or 2 knives. Stir in cream and egg until incorporated. Turn out onto a lightly floured surface
  3. Knead dough for 2 minutes. Press into a 1/2-inch-thick sheet. Cut into sqaures and place on baking sheets.
  4. Bake in preheated oven until golden, about 20 minutes. Allow shortcakes to cool.
  5. Place strawberries in a medium bowl. Sprinkle with sugar; stir to combine. Split cooled shortcakes in half and spread with sugared berries.
